Membership

Join a community committed to advancing the status of women and girls.

As a member of the Zonta Club of Naples, you’ll connect with leaders and volunteers who serve our community through advocacy, scholarships, and hands-on projects. Whether you’re a student, a young professional, or an established community member, there’s a place for you here.

Membership Options

High School

Ideal for students who want to build leadership and serve the community. Opportunities to volunteer, participate in service projects, and grow with mentorship.

  • Service hours & leadership experience
  • Networking with local leaders
  • Scholarship awareness & workshops
  • Join

College / Young Professional

For college students and early-career professionals who want to serve, develop skills, and build a purpose-driven network.

  • Mentorship & leadership roles
  • Professional development
  • Project coordination experience
  • Join

Regular Membership

Community members who want to support scholarships, advocacy, and local service initiatives as part of a global mission.

  • Lead & support service projects
  • Advocacy & community impact
  • Local & international connections
  • Join

Member Benefits

  • Make a tangible impact through local service projects and scholarships
  • Access mentorship and leadership opportunities
  • Connect with a global network through Zonta International
  • Participate in luncheons, fundraisers, and community events

Meetings & Participation

We meet regularly for planning, service, and events. Exact times and locations will be posted on our Events page. Members are encouraged to attend, volunteer, and bring new ideas.

How to Join

  1. Review the membership option that best fits your situation.
  2. Click “Join” and complete the membership form (or send us a message if you have questions).
  3. We’ll follow up with next steps, including dues and onboarding.
  • Ask a Question

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior volunteer experience?
No. We welcome all levels of experience and provide opportunities to learn and grow.

Are there leadership roles?
Yes. Members can chair committees, coordinate projects, and support events.

Is there student support?
Absolutely. We support high school and college members with mentorship and scholarship awareness.
