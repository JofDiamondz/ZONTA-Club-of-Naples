Scholarships

Supporting education and leadership for women and girls in our community.

The Zonta Club of Naples provides annual scholarships to students who exemplify academic dedication, leadership, and service. Awards are made possible through community partnerships and fundraising events, and align with the mission of Zonta International to build a better world for women and girls.

Quick Facts

  • Who: High school seniors, college students, and returning learners
  • Focus: Leadership, service, and academic merit
  • Citizenship/Residency: Local area students (see eligibility)
  • Awards: Multiple awards (amounts vary)
  • Opens: January 15, 2025
  • Deadline: March 1, 2025  (11:59 p.m.)

Applications are now accepted online. Please prepare your information and essays before beginning.

  • Begin Application

Eligibility

  • Identifies as a woman or girl and demonstrates commitment to women’s empowerment and community service.
  • Enrolled or planning to enroll in an accredited post-secondary institution (certificate, associate, bachelor’s, or graduate).
  • Meets local residency/school attendance criteria (Naples/Collier County or surrounding area).
  • Minimum GPA: 3.0 (unweighted) or equivalent. (Consideration given to demonstrated leadership and service.)
  • Submits a complete application by the deadline.

Required Materials

  1. Completed online application (see “Begin Application”).
  2. Two short essays (minimum 200 words each) submitted within the online form.
  3. Optional supporting documents may be requested during finalist review.

How to Apply

Please apply using our online application form. Click "Begin Application" or "Apply Online" to open the form and complete your submission there. If you require accommodations, email scholarships@zonta-naples.org.

  • Apply Online

Selection Criteria

  • Academic performance and persistence.
  • Demonstrated leadership and community service.
  • Alignment with Zonta’s mission and impact potential.
  • Clarity and quality of personal statement.

Timeline

  • Applications open: January 15, 2025
  • Deadline: March 1, 2025 (11:59 p.m.)
  • Review period: March 2–31, 2025
  • Notifications: Early April 2025
  • Awards event: Spring 2025 (see Events)

FAQ

Can I apply if I’m a returning student?

Yes. Students resuming education are welcome and encouraged to apply.

Do I need to be a U.S. citizen?

Citizenship is not required; applicants must meet local residency/school criteria.

Can I submit materials separately?

Please submit a single consolidated PDF when possible. If not feasible, multiple attachments are acceptable.

When will I hear back?

We aim to notify all applicants by early April 2025.

Ready to apply?

Download the application and review the guidelines before submitting.
